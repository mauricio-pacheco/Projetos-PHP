Installation

Uncompress the PH210.zip archive (using such as Winzip) into your phpBB templates directory, making sure that the file and directory structure is retained, e.g. phpBB/themes/PH2/forums/

Log into the phpBB Administration Panel.

Click on Add under the Styles Administration title.

If all is present and correct you should see the PH name listed under Style and Template. Click on Install at the side of the PH name under the Action title. The PH theme should then be available for you and your users to choose from.
